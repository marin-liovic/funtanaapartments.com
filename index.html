Our Apartments

Our apartments are located in a brand new two story house built in 2017 which consists of 3 apartments. You can choose between the studio and the small apartment on the ground floor and the big apartment on the first floor. All apartments have a parking space, air conditioning and free WIFI.

Big Apartment

The big apartment (95 m2) is located on the first floor and can accommodate up to 6 people. The apartment consists of a big living room with kitchen and 2 bedrooms. Top tip: Terrace is the perfect place to enjoy your morning coffee ;)

Small Apartment

The small apartment (51m2) is located on the ground floor to the left and can accommodate up to 4 people. The apartment has a big living room with kitchen and 1 bedroom. Top tip: The couch is the perfect place to rest up until your next adventure ;)

Details

Studio Apartment

Studio apartment (42m2) is located on the ground floor to the right and can accommodate up to 3 people. It has a big living room with kitchen and a comfy bed. Top tip: Jump into the bed after a perfect Istrian lunch and watch TV ;)
